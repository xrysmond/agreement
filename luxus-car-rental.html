01 — Scope

Services Provided by BlaqScale

BlaqScale will design, launch, and manage Google Ads campaigns targeting potential customers searching for the Client's services in Dubai and the UAE.
BlaqScale will install Google Tag Manager and configure conversion tracking on the Client's website to record WhatsApp button clicks originating from BlaqScale-managed campaigns.
BlaqScale will optimize ad copy, targeting, bidding strategy, and campaign structure on an ongoing basis to maximize lead quality and volume.
BlaqScale does not manage the Client's WhatsApp inbox, customer service, pricing, or closing process. Conversion of leads into bookings is the Client's responsibility.
02 — Compensation

Payment Structure

BlaqScale charges 15% of revenue generated from tracked leads. No upfront retainer. No setup fee. BlaqScale wins when the Client wins.

A qualified lead is defined as any WhatsApp button click recorded by Google Analytics originating from a BlaqScale-managed Google Ads campaign, as verified by UTM parameters and Google Analytics data.
Tracked revenue is defined as the total booking value (in AED) confirmed from customers who arrived via a qualified lead, as reported by the Client and cross-referenced with BlaqScale's click data.
The Client agrees to pay BlaqScale 15%% of tracked revenue, invoiced on the 1st of each calendar month for the prior month's confirmed bookings.
Payment is due within 7 days of invoice. Payment method:Crypto.
Ad spend is paid directly by the Client to Google Ads. BlaqScale does not handle or advance ad budget. The Client maintains their own Google Ads billing account.
Minimum recommended monthly ad spend: AED AED 3500. BlaqScale cannot guarantee results below this threshold.
03 — Tracking & Verification

How Leads Are Tracked

BlaqScale will install Google Tag Manager on the Client's website. The Client grants BlaqScale access to do so and agrees not to remove or modify these tracking scripts without written notice.
All Google Ads traffic driven by BlaqScale will use unique UTM parameters. WhatsApp button clicks from this traffic are recorded as conversion events in Google Analytics. This data is BlaqScale's sole property and constitutes the official lead count.
The Client agrees to provide a monthly booking report by the 5th of each month, listing all bookings confirmed from WhatsApp conversations initiated via BlaqScale campaigns. Report format: WhatsApp screenshot or written summary including: date, car/service booked, and booking value in AED.
If the Client's reported booking revenue is more than 40% below what BlaqScale's click volume would reasonably suggest — based on industry-standard conversion benchmarks — BlaqScale reserves the right to request a third-party audit or terminate the agreement with immediate effect.
Intentional misrepresentation of booking data constitutes a material breach of this agreement and entitles BlaqScale to claim the full estimated revenue owed based on click data and average booking value at the time of breach.

05 — Responsibilities

Mutual Obligations

BLAQSCALE COMMITS TO:
Launch campaigns within 7 business days of receiving full access.
Provide monthly performance reports including: impressions, clicks, click-through rate, cost per click, and total qualified leads generated.
Notify the Client immediately of any significant changes to campaign structure, budget usage, or performance anomalies.
Maintain full confidentiality of all Client business data, revenue figures, and booking information.

CLIENT COMMITS TO:
Respond to leads (WhatsApp messages) within a reasonable time — recommended within 2 hours during business hours.
Maintain an active Google Ads billing method with sufficient budget as agreed.
Not run parallel Google Ads campaigns targeting the same keywords during this agreement without BlaqScale's written consent, as this creates attribution conflicts.
Submit accurate monthly booking reports by the agreed deadline.
06 — Exclusivity & Conflicts

Attribution & Conflict of Interest

The Client agrees not to engage another Google Ads agency or freelancer for the same service category during the term of this agreement. Running competing campaigns creates tracking conflicts that make accurate attribution impossible, voiding BlaqScale's ability to verify performance.
BlaqScale may work with other clients in the same industry vertical unless an exclusivity clause is separately negotiated and signed.
07 — Termination

Ending the Agreement

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice via WhatsApp or email.
Upon termination, the Client must settle all outstanding invoices within 7 days. Any revenue from leads generated prior to termination date remains subject to the 15% fee.
BlaqScale may terminate immediately and without notice if: (a) the Client misrepresents booking data, (b) access is revoked without notice, (c) ad spend drops to zero for 14+ consecutive days without communication.
Upon termination, all Google Ads accounts, Analytics accounts, and Tag Manager containers remain the Client's property. BlaqScale removes its own access within 48 hours of final payment.
08 — Liability

Limitations

BlaqScale does not guarantee a specific number of leads, bookings, or revenue. Digital advertising performance is subject to market conditions, competition, seasonality, and ad platform changes beyond BlaqScale's control.
BlaqScale is not liable for lost revenue resulting from the Client's failure to respond to leads, technical downtime of the Client's website, or WhatsApp service interruptions.
BlaqScale's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the 30 days prior to the event giving rise to the claim.
